About us

Just a regular couple from Bristol, UK. We travel, cook, hike, attempt to grow our own food and also keep quail. We are pretty much fully fledged members of the VW owners club as we have a Silver Bug and Yellow T4 called Ellie, which we are in the process of converting to a camper van.

This site is purely for us to use as a diary for keeping track or our travels and adventures but hopefully there will be some useful info on here for other people too.
